Duties

Every day, I conduct patrols around the cave area, inspect visitor pathways, and ensure all facilities are safe to use.

Areas such as Gua Kelam 2 are considered high-risk, so we closely monitor visitors and limit access when necessary.

In case of emergencies, we carry out rescue operations and contact emergency services if needed.

Challenges & Advice

The main challenges include dealing with visitors who do not follow rules and unpredictable weather conditions that make paths slippery.

The most challenging visitors are those who ignore safety instructions.

Advice: wear proper footwear, follow instructions, and avoid entering the cave alone.

The most meaningful experience is helping rescue visitors and ensuring their safety.
